Unified ledger for asset creation.

We believe complete decentralization at one go is difficult.

Decentralization is easily achieved step by step. For that, we are helping you by creating an automated decentralsied bridge between the Blockchain ecosystem and non Blockchain applications. We are providing a click-based compliant asset creation, reconciliation platform that is easily integrated with Xero, Quickbooks, and SAP enterprise via API

Smart Contract for Your automation.

Streaming of assets from generation and reconciliation is always a pain for business owners and we also understand that every business has a different business theme.

So we build a ledger that is fully customizable according to your needs. You can automate the business workflow through a smart contract which is easily customizable via plug and play API.

Full Analysis for Your Data movement through Open API.

We provide you the explorer whose dashboard is filled up with rich analytics where you can easily analyze the assets creation, transfer, and other activities. Open API also helps in full end to end auditing.

Few industry use cases

on top of Zeggo

Fintech companies

SME's/big corporates who are dealing with lots of invoices. Finance person of corporates and founders of SME's can save at least 30 hrs which they spent for reconciliation and doing double verification of expenses.

Trading of securities and bonds are still done on decade-old infrastructure. This can be automated via Zeggo ledger.

Trade Finance

Supply chain companies can build a product that provides the physical-digital binding to track and secure products and parts across the end-to-end value chain. It enables the association of digital records with specific items and global traceability. It is scalable to components of almost every size and shape and can be distributed easily, without risk, throughout your global supply chain.

Automated KYC

KYC is an asset. Getting verified by one organization can be automated and reduce timing for verification from other organizations via our automated ledger.
